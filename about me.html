I am a 19-year-old Egyptian graduate in Information Systems. I worked in a sushi restaurant for two and a half years, during which I dedicated myself to learning and mastering the craft, with God’s grace. Through hard work and determination, I launched my own brand, "Anoos Sushi," an online sushi service that delivers anywhere in Egypt. . My dream is for "Anoos Sushi" to grow and become one of the leading brands in the sushi industry, continuing to expand and succeed. I believe in the power of hard work and dedication to providing the highest quality and unique flavors to my customers. I aspire for "Anoos Sushi" to become a household name and a favorite among sushi lovers across the country. I am committed to achieving this goal with passion and determination.
